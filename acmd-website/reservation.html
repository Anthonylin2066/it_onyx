Cabins

Bear Ridge offers two guests a cozy, fully furnished cabin for a romantic getaway in upper Buffalo River country. Just a short drive to the Ponca Access and the Lost Valley Trailhead. This cabin is conveniently located near the Buffalo Outdoor Center store and the Ponca Elk Education Center.

Amenities Include:
  • Hot tub and stone firepit on patio
  • Wifi
  • 42” TV with satellite/local channels, BluRay player, movies
  • Fully-equipped kitchen – microwave, dishwasher, coffeemaker, toaster
  • Outdoor gas grill
  • Mini-split HVAC plus electric “fireplace” heater
  • Firewood for outdoor fire pit
Pricing starts at $195/night
