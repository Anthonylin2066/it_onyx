What We Do

Our Story

In the Spring of 2010, my culinary roommate and I decided to launch a worldwide food blog to inform all the food lovers out there about new dishes that should be tried at least once in their lifetime. We both attended Le Cordon Bleu - Paris and have worked with famous chefs such as Wolfgang Puck, Gordon Ramsay, and Marco Pierre White. Now, we want to share the dishes that we’ve experienced with you all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide all foodies with information about dishes from around the world. There are many dishes we believe you’ve yet to discover and adding another tasty dish to your try list is something we value as a food blog. We hope to discover new dishes in the future and share our knowledge with the food world.
